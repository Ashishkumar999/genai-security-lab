# Day 44 - OWASP LLM09 Misinformation

## Objective

Identify potentially incorrect AI-generated information.

## Example

Topic:

python creator

Answer:

elon musk

Result:

[WARNING] Potential Misinformation

## Test Evidence

![OWASP LLM09 Misinformation Detection](screenshots/owasp_llm09_misinformation.png)

## Risk

Incorrect information may lead to poor decisions and operational risks.

## Security Benefit

Fact verification improves AI trustworthiness and reliability.

## Real World Examples

- Hallucinations
- Fake Citations
- Incorrect Medical Advice
- Incorrect Security Guidance

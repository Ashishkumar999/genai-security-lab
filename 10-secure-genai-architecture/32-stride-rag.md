# Day 32 - STRIDE for RAG Systems

## Objective

Apply STRIDE threat modeling to Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems.

## STRIDE Analysis

### Spoofing

Unauthorized users impersonating trusted identities.

### Tampering

Poisoned documents and prompt injection.

### Repudiation

Users denying retrieval actions.

### Information Disclosure

Sensitive data leakage and cross-document exposure.

### Denial of Service

Retriever overload and excessive requests.

### Elevation of Privilege

Metadata filter bypass and unauthorized document access.

## Security Benefit

STRIDE helps identify threats across the RAG architecture before deployment.
